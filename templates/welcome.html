This application provides a phoneme search in ancient Indo-European languages. It is grapheme based. You can enter your search pattern as individual phonemes and combine them with useful abbreviations of important phoneme features. Thereby finding the exact pattern you want with less effort. A detailed description of how the search application works can be found here.
